MatrixHunter Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08 · Applies to: MatrixHunter (com.matrixhunter.app)

MatrixHunter (the "App") respects your privacy. This document explains what information the App handles, with whom it is shared, and your rights as a user. The App does not collect personally identifiable accounts, advertising identifiers, location data, camera, microphone, or contact information.

1. Information stored

The App stores only the following data in the device's local storage (AsyncStorage). None of this data leaves your device and none is transmitted to a server operated by us.

  • @mh:settings — User preferences such as language, sound, haptics
  • @mh:in_progress_session — Snapshot of an in-progress game session, used for resume on cold start
  • @matrixhunter:history — Finished game history (up to 50 entries)
  • @mh:onboarded — Whether the first-run guide has been shown
  • @mh:last_match_id — Identifier of the most recent finished match, used for a home-screen shortcut

All of the above is local-only and is removed when the App is uninstalled or when the user clears the App's data from system settings.

2. Permissions

  • Vibration — Haptic feedback during gameplay
  • Audio playback — In-game sound effects
  • Share sheet — Invoked only when the user explicitly chooses to share a result image
  • Internet — Used only for the third-party integrations described in section 3 below

The App does not request location, camera, microphone, contacts, advertising ID (AAID), or external file storage permissions.

3. Third-party services

3.1 Sentry (crash diagnostics) — optional

Production builds may integrate the Sentry SDK (@sentry/react-native) for crash and error diagnostics. This integration is enabled only when the build-time environment variable EXPO_PUBLIC_SENTRY_DSN is set. When enabled, the following information may be transmitted to Sentry:

  • Error stack traces and the screen name where the error occurred
  • Operating system version, app version, device model
  • Non-identifying breadcrumb events leading up to the error

The App does not send your name, email, location, or advertising identifier to Sentry.

3.2 Expo Updates (over-the-air) — optional

The App may use the expo-updates module to deliver over-the-air updates. When this is enabled in a given build, the App contacts the EAS Update servers to fetch update manifests at launch. This request transmits non-identifying metadata such as runtime version, release channel, and platform.

4. Advertising and tracking

The App does not display advertisements and does not transmit user behavior to any tracking or analytics provider.

5. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. The App does not collect or transmit any personally identifying information, so no additional processing of children's information is performed.

6. Data retention and deletion

All information stored by the App resides locally on your device. You may delete it at any time by:

  • Going to your device's app settings and choosing "Storage → Clear data"
  • Uninstalling the App

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in law, the service, or new features. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised when the policy changes.
